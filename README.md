Scorpio Intelligence is a local research workspace for market observation, review, and decision support. It is designed to help users organize market information into clear, explainable, and reviewable research output.

## Product positioning

This project is a research and analysis workspace, not a trading advice tool or a return-guarantee product.

## Commercial packaging

The product is presented as a commercial portfolio with clear public boundaries:

- entry-level research edition for everyday analysis
- professional research edition for deeper review and validation
- collaborative edition for group use
- private deployment edition for contract-based delivery

Public materials focus on product value, release visibility, and user support. Internal implementation details, delivery mechanics, and private workflows are intentionally kept out of the public site.

## Data and compliance

Third-party data services and local storage components are subject to their own authorization and usage terms. Commercial delivery does not imply any warranty, service level commitment, or investment outcome promise unless stated separately in a contract.

All public-facing materials should avoid operational internals, private module names, build scripts, and implementation-specific terminology.
